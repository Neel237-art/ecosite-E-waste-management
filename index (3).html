Process

How EcoCycle
Works

01📦
Collection

Schedule doorstep pickup or drop off at certified centers across Pune–Pimpri-Chinchwad. We handle all device types — from old phones to industrial equipment.

02🔍
Smart Segregation

ML-powered scanning classifies devices by type, hazard level, and recyclability. Items are sorted into safe processing streams automatically.

03⚙️
Processing

Certified technicians dismantle devices, extract valuable materials (gold, copper, lithium), and safely neutralize hazardous components like mercury and lead.

04🔒
Data Wipe

DoD-grade data destruction ensures complete erasure of all personal information before dismantling. You receive a certified data wipe certificate.

05📊
ESG Reporting

Receive digital certificates of responsible disposal with full audit trails — perfect for corporate ESG compliance, CPCB reporting, and sustainability goals.

06🔄
Circular Re-entry

Recovered materials re-enter manufacturing supply chains. Functional components are refurbished and redistributed — extending product life by 3–7 years.

Why Data Wipe Matters
🔐
Identity Theft Prevention
Old devices contain banking credentials, passwords, and personal documents recoverable from even "deleted" drives without proper wiping.
🏢
GDPR / IT Act Compliance
Organizations are legally required to certifiably destroy data under India's IT Act 2000 and global GDPR. Our certificates satisfy audit requirements.
⚡
Military-Grade Standard
DoD 5220.22-M involves 7 overwrite passes making data forensically unrecoverable. Physical platter destruction follows for HDDs.
📋
Full Audit Chain
Each certificate is logged in our blockchain-backed system, ensuring tamper-proof record keeping for your device's entire end-of-life journey.
